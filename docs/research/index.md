# Research

June-2026 best-practice grounding for Ariadne's architecture. Every committed
design decision should trace to a source here, recorded as a `# research(YYYY-MM):`
note in the [Roadmap](../roadmap.md).

## Contents

- **[Best-Practice Architecture (June 2026)](best-practice-architecture.md)** —
  a synthesized, fact-checked survey of how to build an agentic entity-sensemaking
  app: orchestrator–worker harness design, GraphRAG and multi-hop reasoning,
  heterogeneous MCP connectors, multimodal fusion, and the minimum-viable
  architecture. Produced by a deep-research pass (29 sources, 139 claims, 25
  adversarially verified).

- **[Claude Agent SDK Reference](claude-agent-sdk-reference.md)** — authoritative,
  doc-cited mechanics of the SDK primitives (tools, skills, hooks, subagents, MCP,
  context management, deployment) as of mid-2026.

## How this research was produced

Parallel web searches across six angles, source de-duplication, falsifiable-claim
extraction, and **adversarial verification** (3-vote, majority-refute kills the
claim). Confidence levels and open questions are carried through so unsettled
areas stay visible.
